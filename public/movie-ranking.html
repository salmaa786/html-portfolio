The Best Movies According to Salma

My top 3 movies of all-time.

A Bronx Tale

Loved for its powerful storytelling that intertwines themes of love, loyalty, and morality, set against the gritty yet nostalgic backdrop of 1960s Bronx, making it deeply relatable and profoundly impactful.

Harry Potter

movies captivate audiences with their stunning visuals, compelling storytelling, and the seamless adaptation of a magical world filled with adventure, friendship, and the battle between good and evil.

Devdas

cherished for its poignant tale of love, heartbreak, and sacrifice, brought to life through mesmerizing performances, opulent visuals, and soulful music that deeply resonates with emotions.
